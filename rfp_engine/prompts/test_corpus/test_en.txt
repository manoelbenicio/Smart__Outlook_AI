REQUEST FOR PROPOSAL (RFP)
AI/ML Analytics Platform — Fraud Detection & Customer Intelligence
Global Finance Corp plc

Reference: RFP-GFC-2026-UK-0078
Issue Date: 10 March 2026
Submission Deadline: 25 May 2026
Contact: James Richardson, Head of Procurement (jrichardson@globalfinancecorp.co.uk)

---

1. COMPANY OVERVIEW

Global Finance Corp plc is a UK-based financial services group with operations across 15 countries in Europe and Asia, managing £180 billion in assets under management. The company employs 12,000 staff and is regulated by the FCA (Financial Conduct Authority) and PRA (Prudential Regulation Authority). Global Finance Corp seeks to build an enterprise AI/ML analytics platform to enhance fraud detection capabilities and generate customer intelligence insights.

2. PROJECT SCOPE

2.1 Objective
Design, build, and deploy a cloud-native AI/ML analytics platform that provides: (a) real-time fraud detection for payment transactions (processing 50M+ transactions/day), (b) customer segmentation and lifetime value prediction, and (c) regulatory reporting automation (MiFID II, PSD2 compliance).

2.2 Deliverables
- ML Platform Architecture Document (approved by IT Architecture Board)
- Data pipeline ingesting from 12 source systems (core banking, payment gateway, CRM, AML)
- Real-time fraud detection model (latency < 100ms per transaction, precision > 95%)
- Customer segmentation model (minimum 8 segments with actionable insights)
- LTV prediction model (accuracy > 80% for 12-month predictions)
- MLOps pipeline with automated retraining (model drift detection)
- Regulatory reporting dashboard (MiFID II transaction reporting)
- API layer for integration with existing applications
- Comprehensive documentation and knowledge transfer
- Production support for 2 months post-deployment

2.3 Timeline
Total duration: 10 months
- Phase 1 - Discovery & Architecture: 2 months
- Phase 2 - Data Engineering & Feature Store: 2 months
- Phase 3 - Model Development & Training: 3 months
- Phase 4 - Integration, Testing & Deployment: 2 months
- Phase 5 - Production Support & Knowledge Transfer: 1 month

2.4 Minimum Team Requirements
- 1 Lead Data Scientist / ML Engineer (PhD preferred, 8+ years experience)
- 2 Senior Data Scientists (MSc minimum, specialisation in NLP or time-series)
- 2 Data Engineers (experience with Spark, Kafka, Airflow)
- 1 MLOps Engineer (experience with Kubernetes, MLflow, CI/CD)
- 1 Solutions Architect (cloud-native architecture expertise)
- 1 Project Manager (PMP or Prince2, financial services experience)
Total: 8 professionals

2.5 Location
Remote-first with mandatory on-site presence at London HQ (Canary Wharf) every two weeks (Tuesday-Thursday). Travel to Singapore office may be required for Phase 3 (model validation with APAC data).

3. TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS

3.1 Mandatory Technologies
- Cloud Platform: AWS preferred (EU-West-1 London region for data residency)
- ML Framework: TensorFlow 2.x or PyTorch 2.x
- Data Processing: Apache Spark 3.x, Apache Kafka for streaming
- Feature Store: Feast or Tecton
- MLOps: MLflow, Kubeflow, or SageMaker Pipelines
- Orchestration: Apache Airflow or Prefect
- Programming: Python 3.11+, SQL
- Containerisation: Docker, Kubernetes (EKS)
- Monitoring: Prometheus, Grafana, custom model monitoring dashboards

3.2 Preferred Technologies
- Databricks for unified analytics
- dbt for data transformation
- Great Expectations for data quality
- Streamlit or Dash for rapid prototyping
- Experience with XGBoost, LightGBM for tabular fraud detection

3.3 Mandatory Certifications
- At least 2 team members: AWS Machine Learning Specialty
- Project Manager: PMP or Prince2 Practitioner
- Company: ISO 27001 certification (mandatory for financial services vendors)
- Desirable: SOC 2 Type II compliance

4. COMMERCIAL TERMS

4.1 Budget
Total budget approved by the Board: EUR 1,200,000 (one million two hundred thousand euros), encompassing:
- Professional services and consultancy
- MLOps tooling licenses (if not open-source)
- Training and knowledge transfer (minimum 40 hours)
- Production support (2 months)

Note: AWS infrastructure costs are borne by Global Finance Corp.

4.2 Payment Schedule
Milestone-based payments:
- 10% upon contract execution
- 15% upon Architecture Document approval
- 25% upon Data Pipeline completion (Phase 2)
- 25% upon Model Deployment to production (Phase 4)
- 15% upon successful completion of UAT
- 10% upon completion of Production Support period

4.3 SLA Requirements
- Fraud detection model: latency < 100ms (p99), availability > 99.95%
- Data pipeline: end-to-end latency < 5 minutes for batch, < 30 seconds for streaming
- Platform uptime: > 99.9% during business hours (07:00-22:00 GMT)
- Incident response: P1 within 15 minutes, P2 within 1 hour, P3 within 4 hours

4.4 Penalties
- Model accuracy below 90% precision for fraud detection after 3-month stabilisation: 5% discount on remaining payments.
- Platform downtime exceeding SLA: service credits of 2x the downtime duration.
- Data breach attributable to vendor negligence: unlimited liability for direct damages and regulatory fines.
- Late delivery of Phase 4 (model deployment): 1% penalty per week, capped at 10%.

5. LEGAL TERMS

5.1 Governing Law: Laws of England and Wales
5.2 Intellectual Property: All custom models, algorithms, and code developed specifically for Global Finance Corp are the exclusive property of Global Finance Corp. Generic frameworks and open-source contributions remain with the vendor.
5.3 Confidentiality: NDA required. All data classified under FCA guidelines. No data may leave the UK/EU without explicit written approval.
5.4 Data Protection: Full compliance with UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018. Data residency: all personal data must remain within EU/UK AWS regions.
5.5 Data Residency: Strict requirement — all training data, production data, and model artifacts must reside exclusively in AWS EU-West-1 (London) or EU-West-2 (London) regions.
5.6 Liability: Capped at 3x the total contract value, except for data breaches (unlimited liability).
5.7 Indemnification: Vendor shall indemnify Global Finance Corp against any third-party IP infringement claims.
5.8 Subcontracting: Permitted for up to 20% of total effort with prior written approval. All subcontractors must meet the same security and data protection requirements.
5.9 Audit Rights: Global Finance Corp and its regulators (FCA, PRA) retain the right to audit vendor operations, security practices, and data handling procedures at any time with 10 business days' notice.
5.10 Termination: Either party may terminate with 60 days' written notice. Immediate termination in case of material breach or insolvency.
5.11 Non-compete: No restrictions on vendor pursuing similar work with non-competing entities.

6. EVALUATION CRITERIA

Proposals will be evaluated against:
- Technical approach and innovation (30%)
- Team qualifications and relevant experience (25%)
- Price competitiveness (20%)
- Methodology and project plan (15%)
- Client references in financial services (10%)
